Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ant Colony Simulator handles information. The app is designed to run offline and does not collect personal data.

No accounts No analytics No tracking Offline simulation

Summary

Ant Colony Simulator does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The simulation runs locally on your device.

Effective date:

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data (such as name, email, device identifiers, precise location, contacts, photos, etc.).

  • No account creation.
  • No sign-in providers.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No advertising networks.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store basic app state locally (for example, your selected Seed, Nodes, and Edges) so your last setup can be restored. This information stays on your device.

If you delete the app, this local data is removed by iOS.

Network & Third Parties

The app does not require an internet connection and does not send your data to any server.

We do not integrate third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

Permissions

Ant Colony Simulator does not request sensitive permissions (such as location, contacts, camera, microphone).

If a future version adds optional permissions, this policy will be updated and the app will clearly explain why the permission is needed.
