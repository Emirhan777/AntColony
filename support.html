FAQ

How do I change the graph?
Tap Nodes, Edges, or Seed to edit values. Tap a node to move the sugar.

What does the score mean?
Score increases when a green ant returns to the colony (bringing sugar back).

Why do paths “appear” over time?
Ants lay pheromones and follow stronger trails, which can lead to shortest-path behavior.
