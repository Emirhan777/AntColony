FAQ

  • How do I change the graph?
    Tap Nodes, Edges, or Seed at the top. Press Done to apply and regenerate the graph.
  • How do I move the sugar?
    Tap a node in the canvas. Ants keep moving; only the sugar target changes.
  • What do Score and Rate mean?
    Score increases when a green ant successfully returns to the colony. Rate shows the recent average increase per second over the last seconds.
  • Does the app collect data?
    No. The app is offline and does not collect personal data or track users.
  • The simulation looks slow or crowded.
    Try fewer nodes/edges, or change the seed. Some graphs are naturally more congested.

Troubleshooting
